About C418

C418 is the stage name of Daniel Rosenfeld. Daniel was born in Eastern Germany in 1989. He first learned to use a computer at the age of 5, and by his mid teens, started releasing his music on Bandcamp.

He occasionally uploaded music as a hobby until early 2019, when he began collaborating with Markus Persson on an internet forum. Markus was working on a little-known new game called "Minecraft" and needed help creating sound effects and background music.

In 2010, Minecraft was released to the general public and exploded in popularity. Daniel was able to quit his job at an assembly line and work on creating music full-time.

Daniel's music is prominently featured throughout Minecraft, which has grown from a small project Daniel and Markus worked on, to the best selling video game of all time.

Daniel has since produced multiple albums, including music for the Stranger Things series and the imfamous browser game Cookie Clicker.
